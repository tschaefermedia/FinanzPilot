# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Gracefully accepting feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct which could be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to tobias@buildit-consulting.de. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
